Adds a seamless title, content, and excerpt character counter for posts and pages via the admin screen.

== Description ==

Counts the title, content, and excerpt characters while you edit your post or page.

Also comes with real-time SEO-friendly reminder, just in case.

Decon Character Counter overview:

* for title (post or page)
* for content (post or page)
* for excerpt (post or page)
* for biographical info (user)


== Installation ==

1. Install.
1. Activate.
1. Enjoy!
